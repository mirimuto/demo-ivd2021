About.

Every 5th of December is International Celebration Day, a day that marks a unique chance for volunteers and organizations to celebrate their efforts, to share their values, and to promote the work they do in communities throughout the world.

For International Volunteer Day 2021, we celebrate the contribution made by volunteers to build resilient communities across the country by taking over previous campaign of #TogetherWeCanThroughVolunteerim #THANKYOUvolunteers.

Also online talks and discussions involving non-profit organizations, businesses, administrative institutions and individuals will be held on the day to provide the place for sharing their multi-dimensional contributions and impacts of volunteering in response to COVID-19. The key topic in the discussion is to brainstorm for the solutions to better align their volunteer activities and volunteer engagements in post-pandemic world.
